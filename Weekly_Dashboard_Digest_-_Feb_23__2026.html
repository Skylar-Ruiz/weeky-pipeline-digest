Three weeks into Q1, pipeline generation is behind pace across all funnel stages — most critically Pre-Pipeline at just 4% of its $49.8M target. Events are carrying 72% of Discovery pipeline, creating channel concentration risk. The bright spots: branded search is at 149% of target, AI product pipeline outpaces Comms 6.6:1, and EMEA Qualified is pacing at 36%. Immediate acceleration needed across Paid, Organic, and Enterprise.

What's working
Wins & highlights

AI product pipeline outperforming Comms 6.6x
$505K qualified · 19% paced
AI Qualified Pipeline is at $505K vs. Comms at $77K (5% paced). The market is pulling toward the AI offering — a clear signal for GTM prioritization.

Needs attention
Concerns & problem areas
Enterprise Qualified Pipeline — emergency
1% of $10.2M target
Only $24K in Enterprise Qualified pipe. At 1% pacing with 23% of the quarter elapsed, this is the single largest gap in the funnel and requires immediate intervention.
MQL-to-Qualified conversion collapsed
10% (↓41% QoQ)
Down from 17% in FY26 Q4. This efficiency leak compounds every volume shortfall — are MQLs lower quality, or has the qualification bar shifted?
Pre-Pipeline critically behind at 4%
$2.18M against a $49.8M target. AMER at 4%, APAC at 3%. This will cascade downstream if not addressed immediately.
Inbound Paid severely underperforming
~$130K Discovery against a $4.1M target (~3%). Paid Search needs an urgent audit for targeting, budget, and conversion issues.
Channel concentration risk
Events generated 72% of Discovery Pipeline ($627K of $873K). No safety net if event momentum slows.
