Wins, Concerns & Watch Items
✓
Win: HIMSS delivers the quarter's largest single-event pipeline. $3.5M in Opps Created from 42 opportunities — the most productive event by dollar volume this quarter. If even 20% converts to Discovery, it would add $700K and materially close the pacing gap.
✓
Win: CCW Orlando breaks through to Qualified. $620K in Qualified pipeline this week from just 5 qualified opps — a 56% Discovery-to-Qualified rate, the best conversion of any event with meaningful volume.
!
Concern: HIMSS has zero Discovery conversion. 42 opportunities and $3.5M Opps Created with 0 moving to Discovery. At Week 8, this is the single largest bottleneck. If HIMSS stalls, the quarter's target becomes unreachable.
⚠
Watch: Discovery pacing requires 4.7× current weekly rate. $312K/wk average vs. $1.47M/wk required. Even with HIMSS conversion, the team needs to sustain well above historical averages for the remaining 5 weeks to approach 50% attainment.
⚠
Watch: APAC events contributing only 3% of Discovery. With $66K from Spark Delight APAC, the region is underweighted. No current-quarter APAC events are generating meaningful pipeline.
Recommended Actions
1
Launch a HIMSS blitz to move opps to Discovery. $3.5M in Opps Created with zero conversion is the #1 risk to the quarter. Assign dedicated SDR coverage this week, schedule Discovery calls for 10+ top accounts, and set a 2-week conversion checkpoint. Target: 30% to Discovery by April 6.
2
Push eTail Palm Springs through the funnel. $1.7M Opps Created with $320K in Discovery but $0 Qualified. Work with AEs to identify the highest-intent accounts and fast-track qualification calls. The 19% Opps→Discovery rate suggests untapped pipeline remains.
3
Reconcile event costs in CRM. HIMSS, ViVE, eTail PS, CCW Exchange UK, and Sullivan's all show $0 campaign cost — representing $9.65M in pipeline without ROI visibility. Coordinate with Finance to log actual event spend so Q1 ROI can be calculated at quarter close.
4
Capitalize on CCW Orlando's momentum. With $620K Qualified and a 56% conversion rate, this is the closest event to generating Closed Won. Ensure AEs have exec sponsor engagement and proposals in flight — this is the best near-term revenue opportunity in the events portfolio.
