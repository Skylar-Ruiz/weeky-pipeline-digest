Wins, Concerns & Watch Items
✓
Wins
Newsletter open rate hit its highest mark of 2026 at 44.49%, continuing a three-month recovery from February's 34.69% trough — now surpassing December 2025's overall newsletter average of 43.76%. The April AMER newsletter achieved 44.56% open rate and 4.96% CTR on 32,734 delivered, making it the strongest single high-volume newsletter send since January's AMER edition (42.23% on 37,530 delivered). The EMEA edition maintained strong parity at 44.09% open rate, narrowing the AMER-EMEA gap to just 0.5pp — the tightest since January. The Spark #save-your-seat CTA generated 653 combined clicks (AMER 618 + EMEA 35) from the newsletter alone, the second-highest single-link click count in 6 months. Portfolio-level delivery infrastructure holds excellent at 99% delivered. All five KPIs except unsubscribe rate continue to beat industry benchmarks, closing out Q1 Week 13 on solid footing.
!
Concerns
Spark event invites continue to suppress portfolio metrics. The two Spark sends still in the 30-day window (Invite 5 + Invite 6 A/B) account for 36,073 sent emails with a blended 11.0% open rate — 48% of all volume but delivering only 16% of all opens. The Invite 6 A/B test remains the worst performer at 10.07% open and 1.07% bounce rate (190 bounces) — the single largest bounce source in the portfolio. Unsubscribe rate at 0.3% remains 2× above the 0.15% benchmark, unchanged for four consecutive weeks; the newsletter alone generated 126 unsubscribes (0.33%), suggesting it's not solely a Spark problem. Newsletter CTR continues its gradual decline: Feb 6.87% → Mar 4.80% → Apr 4.58% — while still well above benchmark, this is the third consecutive month of decline.
△
Watch Items
Spark event is May 7 — 10 days away. With 10 days until the event, any remaining invite pushes will hit an audience that has received 5-6 previous invites with declining engagement. The question is whether additional volume will further erode list health or whether a final "last chance" framing could re-engage lapsed openers. Q1 closes this week. With Week 13 of 13 underway, the email programme closes FY27 Q1 with strong newsletter fundamentals (44.5% open, 4.6% CTR) but a challenging blended picture due to Spark volume.
